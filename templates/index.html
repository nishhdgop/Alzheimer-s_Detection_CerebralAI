About Us

Remembering tomorrow by predicting today: Your trusted source for Alzheimer's risk assessment

Alzheimer's

Alzheimer's is a neurodegenerative disease that causes dementia, due to the destruction of the brain cells. Diabetic patients are more susceptible to this disease, and this poses a huge problem, as India is unfortunately the diabetic capital of the world with over 70 million suffering from it.

How We Help

We provide an interactive and lucid interface for patients, so that anyone can have access to a quick and free diagnosis of Alzheimer's. Patient requires to upload their brain scans to predict the stage of dementia and receive a detailed report that gives teh diagnosis, via mail. The report is also stored in our google cloud for reference and documentation.

Our Vision

We hope for an Alzheimer's-free India and all other forms of dementia. We want our elders and families to lead fulfilling lives, free from the burden of memory-related problems. We strongly suggest and recommend and early diagnosis of this disease, to help achieve our goal.
